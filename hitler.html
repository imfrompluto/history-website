ADOLF HITLER

Adolf Hitler (20 April 1889 – 30 April 1945) was born in Austria but was a German politician who was the dictator of Germany from 1933 until his death in 1945.

Adolf was born in Austria-Hungary and raised near Linz. He later lived in Vienna but then moved to Germany in 1913.

Hitler rose to power, becoming the leader of the nazi and a chancellor in 1933. He then took the title of Führer und Reichskanzler (leader and chancellor) in 1934.

The Nazi party was a German political group from 1933 until 1945. Under Adolf Hitler’s leadership, the Nazis started World War II by invading Poland. They also made a concentration camp for Jews, Poles, Soviet civilians, homosexuals, disabled people and many more.

On April 30 1945, Hitler shot himself after surrendering when the Soviet troops hunted him down.
